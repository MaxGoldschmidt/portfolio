"Sunny" day in Dublin

Let me just give you a quick introduction about myself. I am a German born in Singapore, but currently living in Dublin, Ireland. So the question "Where are you from?" is always fun to answer.

I am currently working as an Associate Demo Engineer for Salesforce and I mostly do web-development stuff there, although my background is not in Software Engineering at all. I actually hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Loughborough and an MSc in Mechatronics from Bath University (both in England), but I always enjoyed the coding and software engineering aspects the most, which is why I made the switch into the Tech Industry and I definitely don't regret it!

From looking through my website you will probably notice that I'm slightly into photography, but that's not all I enjoy doing in my spare time. I also love playing football and I definitely spend too much time playing Rocket League. So yeah, hitting balls into goals seems to be my thing.

Anyways, please feel free to take a look around this website to find out more about my work experience, projects, etc.
